About me

After gaining a french Scientific Baccalaureate, I went to the University of Exeter where I got my BSc in Biological Sciences, took home a BUCS silver medal and helped found an amazing Student Society. I then went on to the University of Kent where I got my MSc in Biotechnology and Bioengineering, stared in my first student film and took part in my first MMA competition.

Using the skills I'd acquired during my studies, I've worked as a Marine Biologist in Madagascar, I've undertaken genetic engineering and sequencing projects, and am currently working as a Cloud Computing Engineer and part-time Personal Trainer.

And that's just what I've done so far...
